CFA Research challenge

Hello There!

This project is a product of my team's work on CFA Research Challenge 2021. Trying to diverse from other groups we created a comprehensive and engaging report regarding Allegro's financial condition and its potential for the near future.

The project was realized with the use of different tools, such as Refinitiv, Morningstar, Excel, Adobe Photoshop, Power BI, etc. The data was analyzed from a quantitive as well as qualitative perspective.
